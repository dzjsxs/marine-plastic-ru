Product Introduction

Polypropylene (PP) Sheet is a lightweight, semi-crystalline thermoplastic that offers an excellent balance of chemical resistance, mechanical properties, and processability. With one of the lowest densities among engineering plastics (0.90 g/cm³), PP sheet provides outstanding weight savings while maintaining good structural integrity.

PP sheet exhibits exceptional resistance to a wide range of acids, alkalis, and organic solvents, making it the preferred material for chemical processing equipment. Its excellent fatigue resistance and living hinge capability make it unique among thermoplastics. Jiasheng Plastic offers both homopolymer (PP-H) and copolymer (PP-C) grades, as well as glass-fiber reinforced and flame-retardant variants.
