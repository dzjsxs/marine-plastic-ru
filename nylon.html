Product Introduction

Nylon (Polyamide) Sheet, available in PA6 and PA66 grades, is one of the most widely used engineering thermoplastics in industrial applications. PA6 (cast nylon) is produced through anionic polymerization, offering excellent toughness and machinability. PA66 (extruded nylon) provides higher rigidity and better heat resistance, making it suitable for more demanding mechanical applications.

Nylon sheet combines high mechanical strength, good fatigue resistance, and excellent wear properties with the ability to operate across a broad temperature range. Its natural self-lubricating characteristics reduce friction and wear in moving parts without the need for external lubrication. Jiasheng Plastic offers both standard and oil-filled (MoS₂) nylon sheets for enhanced tribological performance.
