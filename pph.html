Product Introduction

Polypropylene Homopolymer (PPH) Sheet is a high-purity, high-performance grade of polypropylene that offers superior chemical resistance and mechanical properties compared to standard PP copolymer grades. PPH is produced without comonomer additions, resulting in a more regular molecular structure with higher crystallinity and improved stiffness.

PPH sheet is particularly valued in the chemical processing and semiconductor industries, where its exceptional purity (low extractables) and broad chemical resistance are critical. Its excellent weldability enables the fabrication of complex chemical tanks, vessels, and duct systems. The material's resistance to stress cracking and good long-term creep resistance make it suitable for structural applications in corrosive environments.
