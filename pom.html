Product Introduction

Polyoxymethylene (POM) Sheet, also known as Acetal or Delrin®, is a high-performance engineering thermoplastic renowned for its exceptional dimensional stability, high stiffness, and excellent machinability. POM is available in two forms: POM-H (homopolymer, e.g., Delrin®) and POM-C (copolymer), each offering distinct performance characteristics.

POM sheet exhibits a unique combination of mechanical properties: high tensile and compressive strength, excellent fatigue resistance, and outstanding creep resistance under sustained loads. Its low moisture absorption (< 0.2%) ensures consistent dimensional accuracy even in humid environments, making it ideal for precision mechanical components. The material's natural lubricity and wear resistance make it a preferred choice for gears, bearings, and sliding components.
